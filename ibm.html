Cyber Report Portal Centre

Cyber Report Portal is established in the year 2024 by the authority of Government of India under the Ministry of Telecommunications and Internal Affairs to reduce the risks of Cybersecurity.

To minimize the risks of cybersecurity and empower citizens with the new technology trends, Government of The Republic of India has drafted a scheme in which the citizens of India will get immediate response from the cyber officials, in case of mobile theft.
